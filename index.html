Reflection

Reflect on the Experience and going foward

"Before intro to Web Programming, I had little to no experience with web development. In addition to not having much web programming experience, I unfortunately was also lacking essential knowledge and experience in UX/UI design. Throughout my academic pursuit of CS, UI/UX design was something I feared and felt would hold me back. However after taking CSIS 1430, I have not only gain the foundational knowledge of web programming, but also I have gain the knowledge and experience of UI/UX. This class has done so much for me as a developer and I’m excited to further integrate all the skills that I have learned from this class into all aspects of my Computer Science Career. "
